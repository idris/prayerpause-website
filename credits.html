Inspiration

Prayer Pause was inspired by the many wonderful prayer time apps that came before it. I am grateful to their creators for paving the way.

If you'd like to consider a different app, I strongly recommend Pray Watch by IslamiCity.

Open Source

Prayer Pause is built with the help of open source software. I am grateful to the developers and maintainers of these projects.

Special thanks to the Adhan library by Batoul Apps for providing accurate, well-tested prayer time calculations.
