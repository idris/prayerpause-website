Privacy Policy — Sukoon

Effective date: January 1, 2026

Sukoon is designed to respect your privacy. This app does not collect personal data.

Data Collection

By default, Sukoon does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. All app data remains on your device.

Anonymous Usage Analytics (Optional)

You may choose to opt in to sharing anonymous usage analytics to help improve the app.

If you opt in:

  • Anonymous usage events may be collected
  • Analytics are processed by PostHog
  • No personally identifiable information is collected
  • No name, email, or account information is collected
  • No location data is ever transmitted
  • Analytics cannot be used to identify you

If you do not opt in, no analytics data leaves your device.

You can change this setting at any time in the app.

Location Data

Sukoon may use your device location to calculate prayer times.

Location data:

  • Is processed only on your device
  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never transmitted to analytics or servers
  • Is never stored by the developer

iCloud Sync (Optional)

You may choose to enable iCloud sync for prayer history and app settings.

If enabled:

  • Prayer history and settings are stored in your private iCloud account
  • Data is managed by Apple iCloud
  • The developer cannot access your iCloud data
  • Only prayer history and settings are synced

If iCloud sync is disabled, this data remains only on your device.

Third-Party Services

If you opt in to analytics, anonymous usage data is processed by:

  • PostHog — https://posthog.com/privacy

If you enable iCloud sync, data is stored by:

  • Apple iCloud — https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Your Choices

You can at any time:

  • Disable analytics sharing
  • Disable iCloud sync
  • Delete the app to remove all local data
